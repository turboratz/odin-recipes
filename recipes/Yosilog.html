Chicken Adobo

YOsi + SInangag + itLOG = YOSILOG!!! A favorite delicacy in Filipino households especially in the urban poor areas also known as squatters area. This dish can be made with variations on the cigarette to be used such as Marloboro Pula for the titos and titas, Marlboro Blue or Black for the Millenials and Gen Zs,or Winston Haba for the hampaslupas!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 3 sticks to 1 pac of cigarettes, depending of addicted you are to chainsmoking
  • 1 kaldero of kaning bahaw
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Directions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute the minced garlic then add the kaning bahaw. Pour in soy sauce to taste
  2. Fry the egg sunny-side-up style or scrambled egg or whatever way you want
  3. You can fry the cigarttes until golden brown or serve it as it is!
